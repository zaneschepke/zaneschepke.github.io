## About

> I started my software development career working for General Motors in 2017. I left General Motors in mid-2023 to work on passion projects. 


Over my career, I have worked on many different projects that span the gamut of software development. I have worked on things like desktop automation by building bots with tools like SikuliX, Selenium, and Nutjs, all the way to full-stack application development for customer-facing e-commerce sites. For many years, my primary focus was full-stack development with technologies like React, Spring/Quarkus, Nodejs, Postgres, and Docker. I had experimented with a mobile application or two, but these were purely for prototype purposes. It was not until the last year or two that I started to delve into mobile application development. I began learning more about Kotlin and Jetpack Compose (as one would expect when working with any Android code from the past few years). I learned some Flutter and began experimenting with building cross-platform applications. I even learned a little iOS development with Swift. Ultimately, I began to enjoy working on mobile applications, which led to where I am now.
